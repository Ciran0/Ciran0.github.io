Building my Own Computer

From Components to a Fully Functional Machine

Building my own PC was a hands-on project that bridged the gap between software and hardware. The process involved researching and selecting each component—from the CPU and motherboard to the GPU and storage—to create a balanced and powerful system tailored to my needs as a developer and gamer.

Skills Gained

  • In-depth knowledge of PC hardware components and their compatibility.
  • Practical experience in assembling and wiring a computer, ensuring proper airflow and cable management.
  • Troubleshooting skills for diagnosing and resolving hardware and software issues during the initial setup.

This experience gave me a profound appreciation for the hardware that powers modern computing and a solid foundation for understanding system performance.
