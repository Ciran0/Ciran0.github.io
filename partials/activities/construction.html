Working in Construction

A Summer of Hands-On, Practical Work

Working in construction was a formative experience that taught me the value of hard work, precision, and teamwork in a physically demanding environment. It offered a different perspective on problem-solving, where challenges are immediate and tangible.

Lessons Learned

  • Enhanced teamwork and communication skills, essential for coordinating tasks on a busy site.
  • Development of a strong work ethic and resilience under pressure.
  • Practical understanding of project planning and execution from a non-IT perspective.

While different from my IT focus, this work instilled a practical mindset and a respect for skilled trades that I believe is beneficial in any professional field.
