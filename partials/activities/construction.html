Working in Construction

Helping build a house

During the summer of 2024 I spent a week helping a friend of the familly who was building their house themselves. I helped laying tiles and pouring the cement.

What it brought me

  • The experience was really instructive as my boss was building his entire house himself and he tought me a lot about the process of building a house
  • Helped me working in a team under direct authority, following orders and following instructions
  • It was also a really good physical exercise and motivated me in trying to stay fit

While very remote from the subject of IT, this experience working in construction really helped me gain a better understanding of the process of tackling large scale projects like homebuilding. It was also a really good exercise of teamwork and learned ma a lot.
