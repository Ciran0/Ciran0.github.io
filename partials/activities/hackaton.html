The blimp my ride hackaton

Team project about bringing back blimps

The 2024 Hackaton at Ephec was about using IT to find inovations in the environemental sector. The theme of our project was the blimps, how to change their perceptions and trying to build an application to help blimp navigation

Outline of the project

Since we were a team pretty poor in web developer we focused mainly on the communication aspect of trying to bring the blimps back, why they were ecologically pertinent, why they fell out of fashion and what we could do to bring them back. But we also worked on a web application using a weather API to provide wind strenght and direction along the potential path a blimp could take. Our team ended up winning the prize of the most creative solution
